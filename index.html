ABOUT



FØR THE BETTER makes music for those who struggle with mental illness, uphill battles, and broken connections. The band–Craig Stanley [vocals], Jake Pomeroy [guitar, vocals], Kris Rank [bass], Max Lalwani [drums]–drive themselves with ambition and dedication to sharpen their name as FØR THE BETTER.



Brain Lock (2020), the band’s first EP, is an introduction into the life of those who struggle with obsessive - compulsive behaviors. Through diagnosis, members Craig Stanley and Jake Pomeroy both suffer from obsessive-compulsive disorder–also known as OCD. The term “brain lock” is used as a metaphor; when the brain is unable to control thoughts, or actions while in a repetitive compulsion. FØR THE BETTER teamed up with producer Steve Sopchak–credited with several Billboard Top 200 charting albums for the six songs that comprise Brain Lock. The concept EP delivers an open conversation to those on their way to overcoming defeat with great triumph.
